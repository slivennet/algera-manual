# How to cancel a medical examination

[Вижте тази страница на български](https://manual.algerahealth.com/kak-da-otkazha-pregled)

1. Click on menu "Прегледи (Reviews)"

1. Select the examination you want to cancel
   [![How to cancel a medical examination](../images/kak-da-otkazha-pregled-01.png)](../images/kak-da-otkazha-pregled-01.png)

1. Click "Откажи (Cancel)"
   [![How to cancel a medical examination](../images/kak-da-otkazha-pregled-02.png)](../images/kak-da-otkazha-pregled-02.png)

1. Confirm the action in the pop-up window
   [![How to cancel a medical examination](../images/kak-da-otkazha-pregled-03.png)](../images/kak-da-otkazha-pregled-03.png)

1. You will receive a message about the cancellation and possible refund (according to the cancellation policy)
> ![Warning](../assets/img/warning.png) **Important**: If the doctor rejects the request (or you cancel at least 24 hours before the scheduled appointment for video and on-site visits), the previously blocked amount will be refunded to your wallet.
