# Login

[Вижте тази страница на български](https://manual.algerahealth.com/vhod)

1. Visit the website [app.algerahealth.com](https://app.algerahealth.com/)
1. Click [Login](https://app.algerahealth.com/SignIn)
1. Enter your email and password
1. Click "Login"
   [![Login to Algera Health](images/vhod-01.png)](images/vhod-01.png)
1. If you have forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it
