## Steps for conducting medical examinations

1. For an **Online Video review/examination**:
- [Login](https://manual.algerahealth.com/en/login) to your profile at least 5 minutes before the beginning of the examination.
- Open "Прегледи" (Reviews/Examinations) and find the appointed hour.
  [![How to conduct a medical examination](images/kak-da-proveda-pregled-01.png)](../images/kak-da-proveda-pregled-01.png)
- Once the time has come, press the "Видео чат" (Video chat) button – a video chat with the doctor will open.
  [![How to conduct a medical examination](images/kak-da-proveda-pregled-02.png)](../images/kak-da-proveda-pregled-02.png)
- Make sure your camera and microphone are turned on. Allow access to them and press the "Join meeting" button.
  [![How to conduct a medical examination](images/kak-da-proveda-pregled-03.png)](../images/kak-da-proveda-pregled-03.png)

1. For **Online Text consultation**:
- As soon as the specialist accepts the consultation, you can start chatting.

1. For **On-site review/examination**:
- Go to the specified address on time.
- Bring your ID and the necessary medical documents (or attach digital copies of them to your Algera Health review/examination).
