# Logout

[Вижте тази страница на български](https://manual.algerahealth.com/izhod)

1. Click on your profile icon (top right corner)
  [![Profile icon](../images/izhod-01.png)](../images/izhod-01.png)
1. Click "Изход (Exodus)" from the pop-up menu
  [![Logout](../images/izhod-02.png)](../images/izhod-02.png)
1. After logging out, you will be returned to the home page
